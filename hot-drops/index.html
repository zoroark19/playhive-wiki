Starfire Crown

Resembling a pirate, Starfire Crown's custom geometry includes a tiny body and a big head.

First seen during the beta of The Hive's bedrock server, worn by some members of the Hive Team, Starfire Crown is a test costume, and, as such, was never released nor planned to be released to the public.

Trivia

  • The costume has the internal name of hive.costume.mini.piratetest, designating it a test costume.
  • The texture of the costume is an edited version of a Pirate Boy skin.
  • In its early years it was used as part of the nick feature, before eventually being removed.
