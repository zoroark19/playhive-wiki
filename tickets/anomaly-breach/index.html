Anomaly Breach

Anomaly Breach gives players access to many in-game cosmetics, based on the viral web-series, once they've completed all of the relevant quests. It is the Hive's sixth ticket, going hand in hand with the Murder Mystery map "Backrooms".

"ANOMALY DETECTED!!" — Marketplace description

Description

The ticket, largely themed around mysterious anomalies and toxic spills, gives players access to both hub cosmetics (immediately) and unlocks across various games (upon completion of the below quests).

An additional, secret reward can be obtained by completing a short, alternate reality game-type puzzle.

Secret

⚠️ Spoiler warning! This section contains detailed information about the Secret Reward that may spoil your enjoyment of it. Read at your own risk!

If the player watches the light above the Backrooms' portal at the Anomaly Breach, they will notice that its flashing has a pattern. To decipher this, the player will need to know Morse code, a common technique in cryptology and high-speed communication.

Decrypting the message will give the player a six digit code. This is unique to each and every player.

Join a game of Murder Mystery and find the version of the Backrooms map with The Hive Hub visible through the window in the map's centre. Head left and then right to locate the correct computer. Interact with it and input your code.

If the player enters the wrong code, they will be met with the following message:
» ANOMALY PROTOCOL // Access Denied.

Attempting this on a Custom Server will result in the following message:
» QCSI // PROTOCOL LOCKDOWN. SHELTER IN PLACE. TERMINATING CONNECTION.
