Hoverboard Costume

The Hoverboard Costume is a cosmetic bundle created for The Hive's Minecoin store but never made publicly available to players. Its official description reads:

"You can't get much cooler than this. Well, maybe if you were frozen..." — Official store description

The bundle was first logged on March 22, 2019 and reappeared in a second, re-priced listing that was last modified in June 2026 — but in both cases it remained hidden from the live store and was never purchasable by the public.

Bundle Contents

Like most Hive costume bundles, the Hoverboard Costume packages a 3D costume with a matching hub title and avatar frame:

Store Thumbnail
"Super Fruit" Avatar
  • Costume: a custom 3D model with a built-in hoverboard the player stands on.
  • Hub Title: The Speed Demon, displayed above the player's head in hub lobbies.
  • Avatar: "Super Fruit," a purple berry riding a small board, used as a profile icon.

Trivia

  • The bundle was re-listed internally at a lower price (160 Minecoins) years after its original 310 Minecoin listing, but neither version was ever made public.
  • The costume's description notes it only functions on The Hive server, consistent with other Hive-exclusive Marketplace content.
