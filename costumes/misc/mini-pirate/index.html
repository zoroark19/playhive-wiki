Mini Pirate Costume

This costume was first seen during the beta of The Hive on bedrock worn by some members of the Hive Team. It is unobtainable and only a select few players have it, in earlier years it was used as part of /nick before eventually being removed.

Trivia

  • The costume has the internal name of hive.costume.mini.piratetest therefor being a test costume.
  • The texture of the costume is an edited version of a Pirate Boy skin.
