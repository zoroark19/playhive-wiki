Skeleton Costume

Resembling, well, a skeleton, Skeleton's custom geometry includes spindly limbs.

First seen during the beta of The Hive's bedrock server, worn by some members of the Hive Team, Skeleton was never released nor planned to be released to the public.

Trivia

  • Skeleton is one of only three remaining costumes to use the original Hive Beta art style.
  • The costume has the internal name humanoid.hive.skeleton, given before the current naming scheme was adopted.
