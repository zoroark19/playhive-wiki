Hoverboard Costume

Hoverboard was a costume on the marketplace purchasable for 310 minecoins. Resembling a young man in trendy clothing, its custom geometry includes the titular hoverboard.

Although added to the Marketplace on March 22, 2019, Hoverboard was never released to the public. Eventually pulled from the Marketplace, it remains unobtainable to this day.

"You can't get much cooler than this. Well, maybe if you were frozen..." — Marketplace description

Trivia

  • Despite never being officially released, the costume's marketplace page was accessible with a direct link. Players who purchased the costume didn't receive it, and obtained a costume gift instead.
  • Hoverboard is one of five unreleased costumes.
  • Hoverboards were popularized by Back to the Future Part II, a 1989 science-fiction film.
  • Due to the hoverboard, this costume likely would've been one of the few to appear to hop, rather than walk.
