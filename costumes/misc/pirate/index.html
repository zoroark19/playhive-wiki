Pirate Costume

This costume was first seen during the beta of The Hive on bedrock worn by some members of the Hive Team. It is unobtainable and only Partners and Staff have it. It is considered outdated since the release of the Pirate Costume.

Trivia

  • Pirate is 1/3 of the only remaining Costumes that use the original Hive Beta art style.
  • The costume has the internal name of humanoid.hive.pirate before the conventional naming scheme was adopted.
