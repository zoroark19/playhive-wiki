Blink Costume

Blink is a costume on the marketplace purchasable for 310 minecoins. Resembling a stone statue with fangs, its custom geometry includes a pair of wings.

Whatever you do, DON'T BLINK. — Marketplace description

Aside from the costume itself, purchasing Blink grants the player the following items:

Trivia

  • Likely inspired by the Weeping Angels from Doctor Who, a long-running British science-fiction series.
  • Despite the wings, this costume does not grant the player the ability to fly.
  • One of the few costumes without legs, it appears to hop, not walk.
  • She's watching you. Yes, you. Look outside your window.
