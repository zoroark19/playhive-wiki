Hoverboard Costume

The costume was first added to the Marketplace on March 22, 2019, it was never released and eventually got pulled from the Marketplace.

"You can't get much cooler than this. Well, maybe if you were frozen..." — marketplace description

Costume Contents

Like most Hive costumes, the Hoverboard Costume grants a Costume, Hub Title and Avatar icon:

Store Thumbnail
"Super Fruit" Avatar
  • Costume: a custom 3D model with a built-in hoverboard the player stands on.
  • Hub Title: The Speed Demon, displayed above the player's head in hub lobbies.
  • Avatar: "Super Fruit," a purple berry riding a small board, used as a profile icon.

Trivia

  • The costume was delisted but players with a link could open it directly and purchase it. Players who bought the costume obtained nothing and were refunded in exchange for a different store costume.
  • The costume was later pulled from the marketplace by Clank after it was purchased by a few players early.
