About The Hive

The Hive is one of the longest-running Minecraft Bedrock servers, known for popularizing minigames like SkyWars and Survival Games years before they spread across the platform. This wiki documents the server's game modes, cosmetics, ranks, NPCs, and history as maintained by its player community.

New to editing? Check the Rules & Guidelines page before making changes, and feel free to ask questions in the Discord server linked in the sidebar.
